## Description

Ever go out with a friend and wonder whose turn it is to pick up the tab? Whose Turn keeps track so you don't have to.

Add your friends, log each time one of you pays, and the app tells you whose turn it is next. Simple as that.

- Add friends with photos so they're easy to find
- Log transactions with a date, description, and who paid
- See at a glance whose turn it is for every friend
- View full transaction history for each friend
- Swipe to edit or delete anything

No accounts. No sign-ups. No internet required. Everything stays on your device, completely private.

Whose Turn doesn't track spending amounts or split bills — it just answers the one question that matters: whose turn is it?
